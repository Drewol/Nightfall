The following must be renamed to include the appropriate suffix depending on the side you want to use it on, e.g:
    "laser.png" -> "laser_l.png" or "laser_r.png"
    "laser_exit.png" -> "laser_exit_l.png" or "laser_exit_l.png"

This does not apply to "laser_entry_l.png" or "laser_entry_r.png", just choose over the one you want to use

The textures inside the "tracks" folder must be renamed to "track.png", just choose the texture with the correct corresponding colors

The following files go into .../textures/
    "laser_l.png" or "laser_r.png"
    "laser_entry_l.png" or "laser_entry_l.png"
    "laser_exit_l.png" or "laser_exit_r.png"
    "track.png"

Accept all file overwrites
